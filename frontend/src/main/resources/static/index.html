Here's a list of fun facts!

Bananas are berries - Botanically, bananas are classified as berries, while strawberries are not!

Octopuses have three hearts - Two pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body.

The Eiffel Tower can grow taller - The tower can grow up to 6 inches taller in summer due to the expansion of iron in the heat.

Honey never spoils - Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.




A day on Venus is longer than its year - Venus takes about 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis, but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.

Wombat poop is cube-shaped - This unique shape helps it stay in place and mark territory without rolling away.

There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth - With around 100 billion galaxies, each containing millions or billions of stars, the universe holds more stars than there are grains of sand on all the Earth’s beaches.

Sharks predate trees - Sharks have been around for about 400 million years, while the first trees appeared around 350 million years ago.

A group of flamingos is called a 'flamboyance' - Fitting for such bright, colorful birds!

Some turtles can breathe through their butts - Certain species, like the Australian Fitzroy River turtle, can absorb oxygen through their rear ends, especially during hibernation.
